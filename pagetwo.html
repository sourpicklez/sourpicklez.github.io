Indian Pickle Pickles in India

Recipe

1 cup Tindora (Dondakaya/ Kovakkai) , finely chopped 1 teaspoon Red chilli powder 1/4 teaspoon Split Methi Seeds (Methi Kuria) 1/2 teaspoon Split Mustard Seeds (Rai Kuria) 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi) 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing) 1 tablespoon Cooking oil Lemon , juice extracted Salt , to taste)
